Art Works

Candles poured from pure beeswax. One-off pieces shaped by hand. We follow color, texture, and the stories materials want to tell. Our wax comes from regional apiaries; wicks are cotton; finishes are simple and natural. Between pours we carve, paint, and play — small objects that feel good to hold and carry the warmth of the maker. Most pieces are one-of-a-kind, many seasonal. Commissions and custom gifts are welcome — we love working with your colors, flowers, and family stories.
